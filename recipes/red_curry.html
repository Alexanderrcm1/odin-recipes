Thai Red Curry

Description

Red curry with chicken is a classic thai dish that is very tasty with its lovely spices. - The secret to a really good red curry is onion, garlic and ginger - they are what gives the curry its "body".

Ingredients

  • 600g chicken breast
  • 1 shallot
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 300g haricot verts
  • 3 green onions
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 tbsp neutral oil
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger
  • 3 tbsp red curry paste
  • 6 lime leaves
  • 4 dl coconut milk
  • 1 dl water
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 dl coconut creamr

    • Serve with:

  • jasmine rice
  • coriander, chopped

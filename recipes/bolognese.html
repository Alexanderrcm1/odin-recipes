Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti bolognese is a rich sauce made with beef, onion, tomatoes and seasonings, a true italian classic. This pasta bolognese recipe will sure beome a family favourite!

Ingredints

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 500g minced beef
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 3 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 125ml water
  • Chopped parsley

    • Seasoning:

  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Beef stock
  • 400g spaghetti, cooked till al dente, watch packet instructions
  • Basil leaves (garnish)

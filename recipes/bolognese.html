Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti bolognese is a rich sauce made with beef, onion, tomatoes and seasonings, a true italian classic. This pasta bolognese recipe will sure beome a family favourite!

Ingredints

  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, chopped
  • 500g minced beef
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 3 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 125ml water
  • Chopped parsley

    • Seasoning:

  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Beef stock
  • 400g spaghetti, cooked till al dente, watch packet instructions
  • Basil leaves (garnish)

    • Steps:

    1. Heat olive oil in a pan. Saute onion and garlic until onion is tender and fragrant
    2. Add minced beef, stir with a wooden spoon to break up any lumps until the water from the beef dissapears
    3. Blend in the tomato paste and the chopped tomates,stir for 2-3 minutes
    4. Add the water, beef stock and seasoning. Adjust seasonning to taste. Reduce heat and let simmer for 30-45 minutes
    5. Sprinkle in chopped parsley. Serve immediately while hot with the spaghetti and garnish with the basil leaves

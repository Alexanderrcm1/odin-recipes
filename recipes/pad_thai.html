Pad Thai

Description

Its hard finding a restaurant that serves a real authentic pad thai. This recipe is the real deal with all the ingredients of a traditional balanced sweet, sour and salty pad thai!

Ingredients

  • 300g rice noodles
  • 300g tofu
  • 15-20 prawns
  • 150g bean sprouts
  • 1 small carrot, shredded
  • 2 eggs
  • 4-5 tbsp finely chopped peanuts
  • oil for frying

    • Pad thai sauce:

  • 0,5dl light soy
  • 2 tbsp tamarind puree
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp palm sugar
  • 1 dl water

    • Serve with

  • lime
  • chiliflakes
  • coriander
